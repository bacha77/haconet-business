Our Vision & Mission

Empowering Haitian Excellence

The Haitian Community Network (HACONET) serves as the primary bridge connecting Haitian-American entrepreneurs, professionals, and community leaders in the Greater Columbus area.

Our objective is to create a thriving economic ecosystem by offering tools for training, strategic partnership development, and visibility. The Haitian Business Meet & Greet is our signature annual gathering designed to spotlight business ingenuity, create pathways to capital, and construct lasting community networks.

"Stronger Businesses. Stronger Community. Stronger Future."

Our Core Pillars

We build initiatives around five major focal points designed to empower attendees and directory listings alike.

Network

Build valuable relationships, expand your professional circle, and discover peer mentoring opportunities.

Showcase

Promote your business, products, and services directly to the local community and key corporate sponsors.

Learn

Gain vital technical insights from industry leaders, resource experts, and business development panels.

Collaborate

Discover corporate partnerships, joint ventures, and strategic opportunities to grow together.

Celebrate

Recognize entrepreneurial excellence and celebrate the positive social impact of Haitian-American businesses.

Annual Haitian Business Meet & Greet

The HACONET Annual Business Meet & Greet is the premier gathering for Caribbean-American business owners, investors, and local organizations. This high-profile event is structured to amplify local entrepreneurship and provide direct access to crucial resources.

Whether you are an established business owner looking for exposure, a budding entrepreneur searching for guidance, or a corporate leader hoping to partner with Haitian businesses, this event will unlock strategic opportunities.
